THE FACTORIES ACT, 1948 — extract of provisions relevant to rotating equipment.
Section 21: Fencing of machinery — every dangerous moving part, including revolving shafts and couplings, shall be securely GUARDED while in motion.
Section 28: Hoists and lifts — thorough examination by competent person at least once every 6 months.
Section 7A: General duty of occupier to ensure health, safety and welfare.
Compliance evidence required: guarding inspection records, competent-person certificates.
